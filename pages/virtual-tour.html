Living Room

Beautiful living room facing the street. Balcony full of plants. Plenty of daylight to work from home and enjoy your stay in Palermo.

Kitchen

Well equiped vintage kitchen with wood cabinets and all you need to make a home-cooked meal. Breakfast bar to enjoy a conversation while you cook.

Bedroom

Cozy bedroom with the most comfortable bed. Close the persianas to achieve full darkness and get a good night's sleep. Wooden vintage floors give the room a special charm.

Bathroom

We recently renovated our bathroom and it now includes a rainforest shower! You can also soak in the bathtub with your favorite salts and make the most of your stay by relaxing fully.
